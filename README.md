# Welcome to Viewframe
This is the official organization for the Viewframe web app.

Viewframe is an all-in-one platform for managing and creating your YouTube content.

## Features
### Transcription
Easily transcribe your content with one click. Just upload the video and then you will get a professional result in seconds.

### Caption Generator
Take your engagement to the next level with our caption generator. Just upload your video file, click start and you will have a fully captioned video at your fingertips in moments.

### Content Generator
Improve your workflow with Viewframe's content generator. Create scripts and video metadata all from just one place. 

### Astra AI Assistant
Astra is your AI-powered YouTube assistant, helping you discover trending content, get smarter recommendations, and grow your channel effortlessly. Make creating and sharing videos easier, faster, and more fun.

### In-depth Analytics
Keep up-to-date with your channel's latest performance with our analytics tab. Get AI generated insights and complete channel stats to take your content to the next level.

### Calendar
Stay organized and on task with our calendar. Create events to manage everything you need to do as a creator.

### Upload
Upload your videos directly through Viewframe with AI-generated metadata based on your video's content.

## Free Forever
At Viewframe, we believe that keeping your channel thriving shouldn’t be locked behind a paywall. That’s why we’re committed to providing professional-grade tools—completely free—so every creator has the power to grow, create, and succeed without limits.
